Candidate

This is a person that is running to be nominated as a general election candidate for President of the United States.

Joe Biden

Joe Biden served the 47th vice president of the United States from 2009 to 2017 under President Barack Obama. He served as a senator for Delaware from 1973 to 2009. He now is the nominee for the Democratic Party in the 2020 presidential race.
